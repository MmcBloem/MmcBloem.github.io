Privacy Policy for CornScore pro!

Effective Date: 2025/03/30

1. Introduction

Thank you for choosing CornScore pro!. This Privacy Policy explains how our game scoring application collects, uses, and protects the information you provide. CornScore pro! is designed solely to track team names, scores, round results, and game records locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect

  • Team Information:
    You may enter team names and update scores during gameplay. This information is stored locally on your device using Apple’s AppStorage. No team names or scores are transmitted to any remote servers.
  • Game Data:
    The app records game progress including round points, overall scores, and game durations. This data is kept solely for the purpose of enhancing your gaming experience and remains stored on your device.
  • Device Data:
    CornScore pro! does not automatically collect or transmit any personal device information beyond what is necessary for local functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Local Functionality:
    All data (e.g., team names, scores, round results) is stored locally to enable features such as score tracking, round summaries, and scoreboards. This data is used exclusively to provide and improve the in-app experience.
  • No External Sharing:
    We do not share, sell, or distribute any information entered into CornScore pro! with third parties or external services.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage:
    All personal and game-related data is stored securely on your device. We rely on your device’s security features (such as passcodes or biometric locks) to protect this data.
  • Security Measures:
    While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure. You are encouraged to use your device’s built-in security measures to help protect your information.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Frameworks and Libraries:
    CornScore pro! is built using Apple’s SwiftUI and other native technologies. These components have their own privacy practices. We do not integrate any third-party analytics or advertising services that collect personal data.
  • No Data Sharing:
    Since all information is processed and stored locally on your device, no personal data is shared with or collected by third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and the “Effective Date” will be updated accordingly. Your continued use of CornScore pro! after changes are made indicates your acceptance of the revised terms.
